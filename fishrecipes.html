Fish curry

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 30cL of coconut milk
  • 350g of cherry tomatoes
  • 280g of rice
  • curry, salt, pepper

Preparation

- Cook the rice. Drain it.

- In a pan, cook the cherry tomatoes, previously cut in half, with oil during 2 minutes.

- Dice the fich. Put it in the pan, with coconut milk and curry. Let simmer 5 minutes.

- Serve with cilantro.

Zucchini / smoked salmon pie

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 1 shortcrust pastry
  • 150g of smoked salmon
  • 3 zucchini
  • 100g of mozzarella
  • 2 eggs
  • 20cL of sour cream
  • ground cumin, mixed herbs, salt, pepper

Preparation

- Cut the zucchini into slices and cook them in a pan with salt, pepper, ground cumin and olive oil. Let cool.

- Roll out the shortcrust pastry, prick it with a fork and lay the zucchini slices.

- Mix the sour cream, eggs, mixed herbs, salt, and pepper, and pour on the zucchini.

- Add the smoked salmon in pieces on top.

- Cut the mozzarella in pieces and lay them on the pie.

- Bake for 25 to 30min at 180Â°C.
