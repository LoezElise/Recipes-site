Croque-Monsieur

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 50g butter
  • 100g grated cheese

- Lay a slice of ham on four slices of bread.

Black pudding and butternut mash

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 4 potatoes
  • 20cl of sour cream
  • 4 black pudding
  • 1 onion
  • paprika, salt, pepper

Preparation

- Peel the potatoes and the butternut and cup them into cubes

- Cook them in a pan of water

- Once they are cooked, mash the pieces into a mash. Add the sour cream, a knod of butter and season.

- Chop the onion, cook it in a pan.

- Peel the 4 black puddings and crumble them in the pan. Let cook for 8 to 10 minutes.

- Serve a layer of black pudding, covered by a layer of mash.

Pan turkey rice coral lentils

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 2/3 carots
  • 400g of turkey
  • 20cl of sour cream
  • 200g of rice
  • 100g aof coral lentils
  • whole-grain mustard
  • curry, salt, pepper, parsley

Preparation

- Heat the sour cream in a pan with the curry, and the whole-grain mustard.

- Cook the carots cut into slices in a pan with oil. Add the turkey, cut into pieces.

- Once it's cooked, add the cream, and simmer for 5 minutes.

- Meanwhile, cook the rice and coral lentils.

- Once they are cooked, mix with the turkey. Serve with parsley.
