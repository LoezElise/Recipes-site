Zucchini/chickpeas pan and burrata

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 1 burrata
  • 160g of chickpeas
  • 80g of dried tomatoes
  • 2 zucchini
  • olive oil
  • ground cumin, salt, pepper

Preparation

- Cut the zucchini into slices.

- Put zucchini and chickpeas on a hob. Add olive oil, ground cumin and mix.

- Bake for 15 minutes at 210°C.

- Cut the dried tomatoes into pieces.

- Put everything on a plate and add the burrata.

White beans stew

Ingredients (4 peoples)

  • 500g of white beans
  • 100 à 200g of feta cheese
  • 760g of tomato pulpit
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • 2 onions

Preparation

- Cook sliced onions in a pan.

- Add the pulpit tomato, Ajouter la chair de tomates, a glass of water and let simmer 2 minutes.

- Rinse the beans and add them in the pan. Let simmer 3 minutes.

- Add the pesto, mix.

- Serve with crumbled feta cheese.
